RESERVE COLLECTION

Reserve Collection lies behind the library desk. Open and close 🕥 together with Main Collection.

The collection is accessible to all NYU community members -- students, faculty and staff. 😄

Most course reserve items have a RESERVE lable on their spines.

There are also a few items with RESERVE lables, called Permanent Reserves.

The Media Collection with over 1200 💿s also locates in the Reserve Collection (room 400B).

In addition, there are a few music books, scores and art magazines.
