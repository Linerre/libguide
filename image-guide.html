Introduction

The NYU Libraries online Course Reserves or ARES (Automated Reserves System) is the system your professor may use to provide required readings.Take these simple steps to access Course Reserves. (Recommended: Sign into ARes from the Libraries homepage first). Additionally, courses are removed from your profile at the end of the semester.

Accessing ARes

The easist way to access Ares is through the course reserve tab on the homepage of NYU Shanghai library. Use your NYU credentials to log in or register for the first time.

After that, you will be able to see the ARes web interface, also know as Main Menu. Listed in the middle are the courses you are currenly enrolled in.
